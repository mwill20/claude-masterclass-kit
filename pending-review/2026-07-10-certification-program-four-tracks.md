## Draft Station

### Layer A - Index

Anthropic's certification program is now four credentials across three roles:
Associate-Foundations (CCAO-F), Developer-Foundations (CCDV-F),
Architect-Foundations (CCAR-F), and Architect-Professional (CCAR-P). This
course treats them as a ladder where each rung builds on the last; Anthropic
frames them as role tracks. Official per-cert exam domains are not yet
published, so the five competency lanes in this course stay put.

### Layer B - Build

Map this course to your own certification path:

1. Open the certification ladder section in `domains.md`.
2. Pick your next rung and write down which of the five lanes serve it.
3. Check your eligibility: the Pearson VUE page states the program is
   partner-network restricted. Confirm whether an individual path exists for
   your situation and note what you find in your own tracking notes.

You're done when:
- You can name your next target cert and the two lanes that feed it most.
- You have a written answer (even "unknown, partner-gated") on eligibility.
