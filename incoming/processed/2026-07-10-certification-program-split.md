  and https://www.anthropic.com/learn

- How it surfaced: reported by Michael, NOT detected by the system. The

## What changed

The single "Claude Certified Architect" path this course's spine is based on
is now a four-certification program:

| Cert | Role | Level |
|---|---|---|
| CCAO-F - Claude Certified Associate, Foundations | Practitioner | Foundations |
| CCAR-F - Claude Certified Architect, Foundations | Architect | Foundations |
| CCAR-P - Claude Certified Architect, Professional | Architect | Professional |
| CCDV-F - Claude Certified Developer, Foundations | Developer | Foundations |
